How the added features work
• Cycle ID links trades into one wheel lifecycle.
• Open Positions summarizes current stock and short option exposure by ticker/cycle.
• APR uses result ÷ capital × (365 ÷ days held).
• Assignment analytics estimates how often assignment happens and how recovered cycles perform.
• Expiry Calendar flags open positions expiring within 7 days.
• Ticker scorecards show which underlyings are actually productive.
• Risk dashboard estimates tied-up capital and assignment exposure from open trades.
